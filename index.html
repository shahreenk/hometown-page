Visit Houston

Enjoy world-class dining, cultural attractions, and outdoor recreation in America's fourth-largest city!

Top three activities to do in Houston:

Dining Out

It's essential to try as many restaurants as you can in Houston. Their diverse and vibrant food scene is one of the best in the world!

Museum District

The museum district is Houston's cultural heart. Along with 19 museums, it is also home to the Houston Zoo, parks, and golf courses!

Sporting Events

Houston his home to four major league sports teams, including the Astros who are reigning MLB champs! Sports fans will be sure to have a blast at a game.
